Kuya Weng's Digital Kitchen Order Monitoring and Coordination System

Brief Description: A web-based solution developed for Kuya Weng's Restaurant to enhance communication and coordination between cashiers and kitchen staff.

Project Description: The Digital Kitchen Order Monitoring and Coordination System streamlines restaurant operations by enabling real-time order tracking, digital dispatching, ticket number assignment, and centralized status updates. By replacing manual order slips and verbal communication, the system reduces delays and missed orders while improving workflow efficiency. Through its dashboards, staff can monitor orders seamlessly, ensuring faster service and better customer satisfaction. Ultimately, the platform strengthens coordination between front-of-house and kitchen teams, delivering a more reliable and efficient dining experience.
